William Friedkins *The Exorcist* is widely considered one of the most terrifying films ever made. Its unflinching depiction of demonic possession and the struggle of faith against pure evil shocked audiences worldwide. The films raw intensity, combined with incredible performances and groundbreaking special effects, creates a genuinely unsettling experience. Despite its controversial nature, *The Exorcist* has had a profound impact on the horror genre and remains a benchmark for supernatural horror.
