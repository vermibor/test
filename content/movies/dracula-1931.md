Bela Lugosis iconic portrayal of Count Dracula set the standard for vampire films. Directed by Tod Browning, this Universal Classic Monster film captures a chilling atmosphere that continues to influence horror cinema. The films use of shadow and its oppressive silence contribute to its enduring legacy as a masterpiece of early horror. The chilling gaze of Lugosi and his hypnotic presence make this film an essential watch for any horror fan.
