F.W. Murnaus unauthorized adaptation of Bram Stokers Dracula is a chilling and visually striking silent film. Max Schrecks portrayal of Count Orlok is grotesque and disturbing, creating a truly unique and terrifying vampire. The films expressionistic style and innovative cinematography create an unsettling atmosphere that has rarely been matched. *Nosferatu* remains a landmark in horror cinema, showcasing the power of visual storytelling even without sound.
