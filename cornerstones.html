1. Mini electric screwdriver — battery, ergonomics, durability

Summary: Compact electric screwdrivers balance torque, size, and battery life. Ideal for electronics, small appliances, and hobby projects.

Real-world notes: Best for repeated small screws; watch for clutch slip and heat on extended runs.

Battery & charging: We prioritize mAh rating, reported runtime at light/medium/heavy tasks, and charge time. Look for replaceable batteries where possible.

Ergonomics: Weight under 250g preferred; soft-grip zones and balanced center of mass reduce fatigue.

Key specs: Torque (in-N), RPM, battery (mAh & voltage), weight, included bits.
